Jobsnplacements.com is a platform that brings together employers, job seekers and other stakeholders through its digital platform i.e. Jobsnplacements.com, Model Career Centres and Employment Exchanges.


Jobsnplacements.com makes no particular recommendations regarding employers. Job seekers are encouraged to perform due diligence in researching employers when applying for or accepting employment offers.


Employers and job seekers are encouraged to request reference information from each other as needed to establish qualifications, credentials and overall fit between the employer and job seeker/ applicant.
